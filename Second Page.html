Community Service

Community service refers to voluntary work performed by individuals or groups, just like in TCGC, ROTC unit. We help each other to benefit our community as well as our institutions. It is distinct from regular volunteering as it can sometimes be mandated by courts or government programs. Community service can include various activities, such as helping the elderly, tutoring children, cleaning up local parks, or organizing food drives just like it shows the picture.
