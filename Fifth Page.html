Ethical Digital Practices

Online Kindness

Always communicate respectfully online. Think before posting or commenting and avoid spreading negativity. Remember that behind every screen is a real person.

Honesty in Digital Creation

Give proper credit for online content. Avoid plagiarism and always cite your sources. Digital integrity is as important as real-world honesty.

Faith & Technology Integration

Spreading Positivity

Use social media to share uplifting messages, inspirational stories, or verses that encourage faith and moral values in your community.

Faith-Based Apps

Leverage technology like apps, blogs, or podcasts to guide others spiritually, promote prayer, or create online communities centered on faith.
